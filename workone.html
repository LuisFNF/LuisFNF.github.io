LFNF Motion Process

BRIEF

My goal for this brief was to learn something new within the motion graphics spectrum, so I was tempted to call this an exploration of different media but during the process the brief changed.

WORKSHOPS

The first weeks of the project I experimented with the different workshops that I had availiable. On the first workshop they showed us how to make a fast storyboard by giving us charcoal and a big paper and telling us to sketch the the video while it was playing non stop. Later we had a workshop where they showed us that we could make an animation by scratching a long film with a exacto-knife and creating frames along the film, so that when it was used in a projector the animation would show.

RESEARCH

The only research I ended up doing was going into Behance and looking at the animation projects there, one of my favourites was "..." and I knew I wanted to learn how to create a liquid line animation so I could implements that on lettering on the future.
